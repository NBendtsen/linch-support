Linch

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Linch is designed to be completely private. It works fully offline and does not collect, transmit, or share any of your information.

Information we collect

None. Linch does not collect any personal data. There is no account, no sign-up, and no login. Everything you create in the app — your rooms, boxes, notes, photos, videos, voice memos, and checklists — is stored only on your device.

How your data is stored

All of your content stays on your iPhone. Linch does not use any server, cloud service, or remote database, and it makes no network requests to send your content anywhere. If you delete the app, your data is removed with it, so we recommend using the built-in Export feature to keep your own backup.

Analytics and tracking

Linch contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not know who you are or how you use the app.

In-app purchases

Linch offers a one-time optional purchase, Linch Pro. Payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details. The purchase is validated through Apple and RevenueCat solely to confirm whether you own Linch Pro; no personal information is collected in this process.

Children's privacy

Linch does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at contact@darkrootdigital.com.
