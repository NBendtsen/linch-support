Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Everything lives only on your iPhone. Linch is fully offline: there is no account, no sign-up, and no server. Your notes and photos never leave your device.

How do I back up my data?

Open Settings and choose Export. Linch bundles all your rooms, boxes, notes, and photos into a single file you can save anywhere. To move to a new device or restore, use Import and pick that file.

What does Linch Pro include?

The free version includes three rooms, three boxes, and one checklist. Linch Pro removes those limits so you can create as many as you like. It is a one-time purchase, not a subscription.

I bought Pro but it isn't showing.

Open Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same account.

I forgot a room's PIN.

A room's PIN is stored only on your device and cannot be recovered. If you forget it, the room can still be deleted, but its contents cannot be unlocked without the PIN.

Privacy

Linch does not collect any data. It makes no network requests for your content, has no analytics, and has no accounts. The only exception is the in-app purchase, which is handled entirely by Apple.
